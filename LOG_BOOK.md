Data is highly biased towards anustubh meter.

1. Zero Shot experiment done
- Untrained experiment gives a completely expected result. Many outputs were corrupted with english texts and explanations despite instructions in prompts
- After finetuning in zero shot setting, accuracy reached to 54% but all correct responses were anustubh only proving the biasness.

2. 1-shot experiment done
- After finetuning in 1 shot setting, accuracy increased from 54.74% --> 58.27%. And this time apart from anutubh Indravajrā, Upendravajrā, Vasantatilakā, Vaṃśastha.
